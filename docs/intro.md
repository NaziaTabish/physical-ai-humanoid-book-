# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics – Essentials

Welcome to the "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics – Essentials" textbook. This comprehensive resource provides a concise, professional introduction to the fundamentals of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics.

## About This Textbook

This textbook is designed for:
- Beginners seeking to understand the basics of Physical AI
- Intermediate learners looking to deepen their knowledge
- Anyone interested in the intersection of artificial intelligence and robotics

## What You'll Learn

Through six structured chapters, you'll explore:
1. The foundations of Physical AI and how it differs from virtual AI
2. Core robotics systems, structures, and mechanisms
3. Human-inspired design principles in robotics
4. Perception systems used in humanoid robots
5. The role of AI and deep learning in control systems
6. Principles of humanoid locomotion and manipulation

## Navigation

Use the sidebar to navigate between chapters. Each chapter is designed to be self-contained while building upon previous concepts.
